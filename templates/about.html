About MLCrop Recommender

What is MLCrop Recommender?

The MLCrop Recommender is a Crop Recommendation System that uses machine learning to predict the most suitable crop for your specific soil and climate conditions. The system analyzes seven key parameters to provide accurate recommendations.

How does it work?

The system uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on agricultural data to analyze:

  • Soil Nutrients: Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K)
  • Soil Chemistry: pH level
  • Climate Factors: Temperature, Humidity, Rainfall
